Overview

Purpose

My purpose to this website is to give users the ability to find great nonfiction and fiction books when stuck in between series. Additionally, I will be better able to keep track of all the previous books I have read and my thoughts on them.

Audience

My audience will obviously be avid book readers. The target age range will be roughly 12 to 30 years old. Because of the scope of my genres, this will be primarily aimed at younger aged fiction books and adults who are interested in self-improvement
